The Big Event

The Big Event is a nonprofit Aggie run organization that gives back to the community of Bryan/College Station every year. They are the largest service event hosted at Texas A&M and I have been a part of it for 3 years now. I have done everything from mulching and weeding, to replacing a porch.

NRH Animal Rescue Center

The NRH Animal Rescue Center is a shelter for animals that have been rescued within the North Richlands Hills area. I volunteered there in prior years and have tried to help out whenever I go back home.

Salvation Army

I have volunteered for The Salvation Army a few times throughout my college career. I had done it with my student organization AFC throughout the two years I was a part of the organization. I plan to help out more with them in the future along with many other organizations.
